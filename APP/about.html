What is AgriGuide?

AgriGuide is a smart application designed for farmers, offering them a personal space to monitor the status of their crops. It provides recommendations based on soil moisture, weather data, and crop type. With its features, AgriGuide helps optimize irrigation, plan farming activities, and improve yields.

Using AgriGuide means adopting smarter and more efficient agriculture. With its personalized advice, farmers can better manage irrigation, avoid resource waste, and make decisions based on accurate data. This not only increases productivity but also reduces risks related to climate conditions and poor farming practices.
